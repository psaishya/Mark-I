Mark I is an innovative initiative that aims to use computer vision technology to transform how we interact with digital content. Computer Vision, a field of Artificial Intelligence (AI), allows computers and systems to extract meaningful information from digital images, videos, and other visual inputs, and take actions or make recommendations based on this information.

Mark I offers unique canvas where users can draw without physically touching any input devices. Leveraging the power of computer vision, users interact with the canvas using just the tip of their finger. Simply by showing their hand in front of a web camera, the system detects the user's palm and tracks 21 different landmarks on it. The user can then interact with the canvas using a hand gesture similar to that of writing.
